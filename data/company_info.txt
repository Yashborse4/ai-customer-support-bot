# Acme Corp Support Documentation

## Product: SuperWidget 3000
The SuperWidget 3000 is our flagship smart home controller. It supports Zigbee, Z-Wave, and Matter protocols.

### Common Troubleshooting
1. **Device not connecting**: Ensure the SuperWidget is within 30 feet of your router. Reset the device by holding the button for 10 seconds.
2. **Firmware Update**: Firmware updates are pushed automatically every Tuesday at 2 AM. Do not disconnect power during updates.
